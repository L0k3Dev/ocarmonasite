About Me

Gameplay programmer with experience in C++ and Blueprints in Unreal Engine. I’ve worked on movement, combat, targeting systems, animation logic, and internal tools – both in personal projects and released titles.

I enjoy building clean, modular systems and improving existing ones when possible. I’m used to working in multidisciplinary teams and I always try to leave things better than I found them.

Recently, I've contributed to the release of 'Magical Bakery' and 'Petit Island' (UE5/4.27), and developed AI, VFX, and gameplay systems in multiple game jams and indie projects.
